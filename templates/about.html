About us

We are students of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, driven by a shared passion to innovate and make a positive impact. With a keen interest in accessibility and communication, we are dedicated to building technologies that bridge the gap between spoken language and sign language. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions, such as speech-to-sign and sign-to-speech systems, to empower individuals with hearing impairments and facilitate seamless communication. Through our collaborative efforts and commitment to excellence, we strive to create inclusive environments where everyone has the opportunity to express themselves and be understood.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where communication barriers between individuals who use sign language and those who rely on spoken language are completely eliminated. We envision a future where everyone, regardless of their hearing ability, can communicate seamlessly, fostering inclusivity and understanding in all aspects of life.
